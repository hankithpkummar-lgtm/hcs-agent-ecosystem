- **Auto-Trigger System** — Activates on whatsapp marketing keywords
- **9-Stage Marketing Pipeline** — Complete marketing workflow
- **Link Analysis** — Auto-detect and categorize links
- **Message Generation** — Professional message templates
- **One-Click WhatsApp** — Open WhatsApp with pre-filled message
